COACHING AND EXAMINATION

Comprehensive preparation for UPSC Civil Services Examination.

Preliminary Examination:

Based on merit (through a competitive entrance Examination conducted throughout Tamil Nadu) and Community Reservation system, 225 aspirants are admitted in Residential coaching (Full-time) and 100 aspirants in Non-Residential (Part-time) coaching. From the year 2011-2018 onwards, ICR applications are issued to the aspirants through District Collectorate. The entrance examination is conducted in seventeen (17) centres in Tamil Nadu.

Now the entrance examination is conducted directly by All India Civil Services Centre, Chennai-28. When the entrance examination is conducted for preliminary coaching, notifications will be released on the website and newspaper advertisement also published. Except admission, other processing (application, hall ticket downloading, selection list, admit card) will be held online only.

Preliminary Examination Pattern:
Paper I (Maximum Marks 200) Paper II (Maximum Marks 200)
General Studies CSAT (qualifying nature, 33% pass percentage not included for ranking)

The Institute guides aspirants for nearly 6 months (December to June) to take up the Preliminary examination.

Main Examination:

The aspirants who have cleared the UPSC Preliminary examination are admitted for Main Examination Coaching (225 aspirants). Intensive coaching for nearly two to four months is given to the aspirants. A monthly stipend of Rs. 3000/- is given to each candidate admitted for Main coaching, irrespective of the income ceiling to meet expenditure for accessing reading materials.

Personality Test:

The Institute conducts Model Personality Tests for candidates who have cleared their Main Examination. Sessions are conducted by senior officers and subject experts. Topics are discussed with experts. Accommodation is available in Tamilnadu House, New Delhi, with Rs. 2000/- travel allowance provided for attendance.
