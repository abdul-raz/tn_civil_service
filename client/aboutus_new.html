    Our Institution

    In the year 1966 Pre-Examination Training Centre was established to train SC / ST candidates to take up Civil Services Examination. Similarly in 1971, a Special Training Institute was established to guide Backward Class and Most Backward Class candidates. Both the Institutes were merged and renamed as "ALL INDIA CIVIL SERVICES COACHING CENTRE" and is headed by the Principal and functions under the control of Director, Anna Administrative Staff College and Director General of Training from February, 2000 onwards.
