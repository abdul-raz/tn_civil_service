  • Provisional list is based on your Naan Mudhalvan score card.

  • Provisional selection by AICSCC does not entitle you to Naan Mudhalvan stipend.

CLARIFICATION: Provisionally selected candidates will be admitted first. If vacancies arise, a waiting list will be announced on 20.10.2023 with a certificate verification schedule.

  • Hostel room will be provided at AICSCC, Chennai (Full time) on certificate verification day if all certificates are submitted and Rs.3,000/- deposit remitted.
  • Accommodation at ACCSCA, Madurai and Coimbatore is given only on the day classes start (30.10.2023).

CLARIFICATION: You must attend on the allotted dates, but exceptions will be considered with proof if you have another exam/interview.
