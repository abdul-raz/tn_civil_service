COACHING

Coaching And Examination

Comprehensive preparation for UPSC Civil Services Examination.

Preliminary Examination

Based on merit through a competitive entrance examination conducted throughout Tamil Nadu and Community Reservation system, 225 aspirants are admitted in Residential coaching and 100 aspirants in Non-Residential coaching.

Main Examination

The aspirants who have cleared the UPSC Preliminary examination are admitted for Main Examination Coaching (225 aspirants). Intensive coaching for nearly two to four months is given to the aspirants.

Personality Test

The Institute conducts Model Personality Tests for candidates who have cleared their Main Examination. Sessions are conducted by senior officers and subject experts.

Preliminary Examination

Every year an entrance examination is conducted by All India Civil Services Coaching Centre for the selection of 325 eligible candidates (225 Residential and 100 Non-residential) for admission to Preliminary Examination Coaching. Every year during the month of August/September advertisement for entrance exam is being given in the newspapers and also in official website www.civilservicecoaching.com entrance exam will be conducted during the month of September/October.

The selection is made based on the marks secured by the candidates in Entrance Examination. The Institute guides the candidates for nearly Nine months, (September to May) to take up the Preliminary examination. The aspirants appearing in Entrance Examination, this year 2024-2025 Applications are accepted through the online portal on our official website. In association with Nan Muthalvan scheme, All India Civil Services Coaching Centre conducted Entrance Test or Preliminary Examination coaching 26,587 applications were received, 18,405 students wrote entrance examination.

(220(Full Time) + (part time)) students have been selected based on the entrance test. Additionally, (95 Madurai+95 Coimbatore) individuals are undergoing training for the Preliminary Examinations at the Anna Centenary Civil Services Coaching Centers in both Madurai and Coimbatore.

Preliminary Examination Pattern:
Paper I (Maximum Marks 200) Paper II (Maximum Marks 200)
General Studies CSAT (qualifying nature, 33% pass percentage not included for ranking)

Duration: The Institute guides aspirants for nearly 6 months (December to June) to take up the Preliminary examination.

Main Examination

The candidates who clear the Preliminary examination are given intensive coaching for nearly three months (from July to September) to face the Main examination. The candidates not only from this institute but also other candidates belonging to Tamilnad who clears the Preliminary examination are admitted for main examination coaching in this institute.

Every year, 225 candidates were given coaching for the Main examination. A monthly stipend of Rs.3000/- per month for three months is given to all the 225 candidates admitted for Main coaching, irrespective of the income ceiling to meet the expenditure for accessing reading materials.

In co-ordination with the Nan Muthalvan Scheme, 216 aspirants received coaching for the UPSC main examination 2024. Each of these 216 aspirants was provided a stipend of Rs 25,000 as part of the initiative. 203 mains aspirants received Rs.9000 for three months. 48 aspirants have cleared the UPSC main exam in 2024.

Personality Test

The Institute conducts Model Personality Test for the candidates who come out successfully in the Main Examination. The above test is being conducted well in advance before the actual date of Personality Test held at New Delhi. As per Human resources Management (Training-1) Dept, G.O(Ms) No.68, Dt: 08.07.2022 the incidental expenses has been increased from Rs.2000/ to Rs.5000/-.

The training center facilitated Model personality test, 69 aspirants participated on December 18-20, 2024, providing each with Rs. 5,000 for Delhi travel expenses to attend the subsequent official personality test. Following this, 17 individuals participated to the second phase of the personality test held on February 27, 2025, resulting in a total of 86 participants benefiting from the program.

Key Features:
  • Mock interviews with senior officers
  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group discussions and presentations
  • Current affairs and subject-specific discussions
  • Communication skills enhancement

Support Provided: Accommodation is available in Tamilnadu House, New Delhi, with Rs. 5,000/- travel allowance provided for attendance.
