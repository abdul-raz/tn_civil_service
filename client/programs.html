    Coaching And Examination

    Comprehensive preparation for UPSC Civil Services Examination.

    Preliminary Examination

    Based on merit through a competitive entrance examination conducted throughout Tamil Nadu and Community Reservation system, 225 aspirants are admitted in Residential coaching and 100 aspirants in Non-Residential coaching.

    Main Examination

    The aspirants who have cleared the UPSC Preliminary examination are admitted for Main Examination Coaching (225 aspirants). Intensive coaching for nearly two to four months is given to the aspirants.

    Personality Test

    The Institute conducts Model Personality Tests for candidates who have cleared their Main Examination. Sessions are conducted by senior officers and subject experts.

    Preliminary Examination

    From the year 2011-2018 onwards, ICR applications are issued to the aspirants through District Collectorate. The entrance examination is conducted in seventeen (17) centres in Tamil Nadu.

    Now the entrance examination is conducted directly by All India Civil Services Centre, Chennai-28. When the entrance examination is conducted for preliminary coaching, notifications will be released on the website and newspaper advertisement also published. Except admission, other processing (application, hall ticket downloading, selection list, admit card) will be held online only.

    Preliminary Examination Pattern:
    Paper I (Maximum Marks 200) Paper II (Maximum Marks 200)
    General Studies CSAT (qualifying nature, 33% pass percentage not included for ranking)

    Duration: The Institute guides aspirants for nearly 6 months (December to June) to take up the Preliminary examination.

    Main Examination

    A monthly stipend of Rs. 3000/- is given to each candidate admitted for Main coaching, irrespective of the income ceiling to meet expenditure for accessing reading materials.

    Main Examination Pattern:
    Papers Marks Total Marks
    Language --- (qualifying)
    English --- (qualifying)
    Essay 250 (Compulsory) 250
    General Studies – Paper I, II, III & IV 250 x 4 (Compulsory) 1000
    Optional I & II (Only one optional) 250 x 2 (Compulsory) 500
    Personality Test 275
    Total 2025

    Personality Test

    Topics are discussed with experts to prepare candidates for the interview process. The personality test is crucial for final selection in the civil services.

    Key Features:

    • Mock interviews with senior officers
    • Individual counseling sessions
    • Group discussions and presentations
    • Current affairs and subject-specific discussions
    • Communication skills enhancement

    Support Provided: Accommodation is available in Tamilnadu House, New Delhi, with Rs. 2000/- travel allowance provided for attendance.
