Admission Process

How to Join AICSCC

Simple and transparent admission process for aspiring civil servants

1

Application Submission

Submit your online application with all required documents including educational certificates, domicile proof, and income certificate.

2

Entrance Examination

Appear for the entrance test covering general aptitude, reasoning, and current affairs. Merit-based selection process.

3

Document Verification

Physical verification of all submitted documents and certificates by our verification committee.

4

Final Selection & Enrollment

Merit list publication and enrollment process completion. Welcome to the AICSCC family!
