Basic Eligibility Requirements

The candidate must hold a degree of any recognized university.
Age Limit(as on 1st August):Minimum: 21 years for all candidates Maximum: 32 years
The upper age limit prescribed above will be relaxable: Upto a maximum of Five years if a candidate belongs to SC/ST/SC(A) Upto a maximum of Three years in the case of candidates belonging to to BC/BC(M)/MBC/DNC
Upto a maximum of Ten years in the case of blind, deaf-mute and Orthopaedically handicapped persons.(General Category)
