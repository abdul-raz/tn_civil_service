Infrastructure & Facilities

Our Facilities

World-class infrastructure for comprehensive civil services preparation

Residential Hostel

A congenial and conducive atmosphere is provided to the aspirants at the Institute with air-conditioned classrooms, library, meeting hall, reading hall, etc.

Accommodation

Accommodation is available at Tamilnadu House, New Delhi for Tamilnadu Aspirants. Travelling allowance of Rs.2000/- is provided.

Reference Library

Aspirants are provided with a well-equipped Digitalized Library with e-books also available and more than 25,000 books and journals.

Computer Lab

Wifi connectivity is provided with 55 computers to train the students.

Medical Care

On-campus medical facility with qualified doctors and nursing staff for health care.

Faculties

Experienced faculty, personalized mentoring, interactive teaching, support for every aspirant.

Infrastructure

Comprehensive coaching facilities designed for optimal learning environment

Modalities Adopted for Coaching

Series of Lectures by subject experts for guidance and initiation into the topics

Counseling sessions

Model Test for Two Months towards the end of the course

Motivational talks will be given by senior IAS and IPS officers and candidates qualified for the civil services who have enter service from this Institute

Seminars and Group Discussions are conducted to improve the communication skills of the candidates

Upto a maximum of Ten years in the case of blind, deaf-mute and Orthopaedically handicapped persons.(General Category)
